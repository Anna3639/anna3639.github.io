Synthesis of Cyclohexene (PDF)
This four-page lab report describes the process of synthesizing cyclohexene from cyclohexanol with an acid-catalyzed dehydration reaction. IR analysis, gas chromatography, and chemical tests with bromine in dichloromethane and potassium permanganate were used to assess the identity and purity of the cyclohexene product.


Isolation of Trimyristin (PDF)
This 4-page paper covers an experiment to extract trimyristin from nutmeg with tert-butyl methyl ether. It was purified through recrystallization used to produce myristic acid through hydrolysis of the trimyristin with sodium hydroxide, ethanol, concentrated hydrochloric acid, and water. Both the trimyristin and myristic acid were identified by their melting points.
